# Sample arithmetic expressions to test the calculator grammar

1 + 2
5 - 3
4 * 6
8 / 2

## Complex Expressions
1 + 2 * 3
(1 + 2) * 3
10 - 5 + 2
20 / 4 * 3

## Decimal Numbers
3.14 + 2.86
10.5 * 2
7.25 / 2.5

## Nested Expressions
((1 + 2) * 3) + 4
(10 - (3 + 2)) * 2
1 + (2 * (3 + 4))
